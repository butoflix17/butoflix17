Read About Us

In today’s generation owning a car is no longer a luxury but a necessity. To keep up with pace of growing economy, people need car to travel to diverse destinations but not ALL can afford to buy a Brand New Car. Grhenloisa Car Trade is a Cebu based Second Hand Car Dealer which is currently located at Uytengsu St. Jones Ave. Cebu City. We always make sure to uphold our integrity and good business practice by selling Quality Cars at Reasonable Prices. We also offer financing schemes to clients, enabling them to have an affordable and practical options. Also, we make sure that the cars being bought from us are in good condition with clean, complete and updated documents so that there is nothing for them to worry about. Being able to give value to our client’s hard earned money is our aim in order to win their Trust and Loyalty. That is why, most of our new clients are referred by our old clients or from people who received good feedbacks from our satisfied clients. We DO NOT only Sell Cars but we buy cars in spot cash and we accept Swaps or Trade-Ins. In summary, Grhenloisa Car Trade is your perfect choice in finding the Best Deals for second hand cars.

We do this by making sure to have a variety of cars that would fit every customer’s needs and expectations. Treating customers as we would like to be treated, being able to give them fair, responsible, transparent, efficient, professional and friendly deals. We will make sure that we will create an environment which fosters continued growth. To recognize the contributions and efforts of our team by encouraging them to improve and together we will succeed.

We aim to win Customer’s Loyalty by giving Value for their money through Reasonably Priced Quality Used Cars and Excellent Customer Service for the the Ultimate Buying Experience
